2by2: Interactive Data Visualization Tool

What if the key to collecting data is also rooted in visualization? In this project, I set out to develop 2by2–a web-based survey tool that allows users to submit data through interacting with a data visualization. Specifically, 2by2 enables users to (1) provide individual data by placing and manipulating points on a graph and (2) visualize both group and individual responses.
